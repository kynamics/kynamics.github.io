About Us

Kynamics was started to make use of AI to make it easier to interface with software. We realized that over time, software kept getting complex and that was reflected in a high nested UI design. This caused problems for most users who would always need help figuring out “how to do certain things”.


Our goal is to build the best on-device voice-based intelligent interface to your every day apps.


Our team consists of experienced engineers who have built voice-based AI at top companies in Silicon Valley. Their products are currently being used by more than a Billion people worldwide.
